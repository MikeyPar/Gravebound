**Exact rules and prototype defaults:**

- Default binding is right mouse (`ability_1`) and is rebindable. Ability presses carry checked monotonic `u32` sequence numbers.
- `100 ms` input buffering compiles round-to-nearest to 3 ticks. `150 ms` global cooldown compiles round-to-nearest half-up to 5 ticks. `5,000 ms` cooldown compiles to 150 ticks.
- A legal ready press fires on the current fixed tick using the latest normalized aim and authoritative player center. Cooldown and global cooldown begin on fire, not impact.
- A press received when readiness is at most 3 ticks away is buffered through that many future ticks and fires on the first ready tick. A press earlier than that is consumed without buffering. A newer legal press replaces an older pending press; stale/regressed sequences fail transactionally.
- Grave Mark fires one bolt at `12 tiles/second`, `11 tile` range, and `0.12 tile` radius. Radius is the unoverridden common `CONT-013` default.
- Range/speed derives a 28-travel-tick maximum. Full steps are `0.4 tiles`; the 28th step clamps to the remaining `0.2` tiles.
- The bolt has no pierce and consumes on the first enemy or solid. `CLS-020` Nailkeeper's “first enemy or wall impact” establishes wall collision; without Nailkeeper, a solid contact emits block feedback and creates no trap or mark.
- Grave Mark raw-damage intent is `1.8W`. With the current Pine Crossbow `W=20`, this resolves once by nonnegative half-up fixed-point math to `36` raw damage. No health is changed.
- Enemy contact applies owner-specific `graveled_mark` for exactly 120 ticks starting on the contact tick. It is active for contact ticks `T..T+119` and expires before projectile collision processing on tick `T+120`.
- Maximum marked targets is one. Marking a different target emits one replacement event; marking the same target refreshes duration to 120 ticks. Solid/range expiry never changes the active mark.
- A Pine Crossbow enemy contact checks the active mark in ascending projectile-ID processing order. If active and target-equal, it emits a primary raw-damage intent with multiplier `11,500` basis points; current `20` raw becomes `23` by half-up rounding. Otherwise it emits the ordinary `10,000` basis-point intent.
- If a Mark bolt and primary bolt contact on one tick, ascending projectile ID decides whether the mark exists for the later contact. No unordered iteration participates.

**Legal state transitions:**

- Ability input: `Idle -> PressObserved -> Fired | Buffered(1..3) | ConsumedTooEarly`; `Buffered -> Fired | Replaced`.
- Cooldown: `Ready -> Fired/Cooling(150) -> ... -> Cooling(1) -> Ready`.
- Global cooldown: `Ready -> Fired/Global(5) -> ... -> Global(1) -> Ready`.
- Mark projectile: `Spawned -> Travelling -> HitEnemy | HitSolid | ExpiredAtRange`.
- Active mark: `None -> Applied(target,120) -> Refreshed | Replaced | Expired -> None`.
- Damage intent: `CollisionValidated -> RawIntentEmitted`; no mutation transition exists in this ticket.

**Rejected inputs and typed errors:**

- Missing/duplicate/wrong-kind Grave Mark record, class not referencing it, nonexact First Playable values, zero/nonrepresentable durations/speed/range/radius/multiplier/target count, invalid default resolution, sequence regression, sequence/ID exhaustion, or non-finite aim/player/collision result fails closed.
- A rising physical press while combat input is blocked is suppressed until release and cannot leak when the gate opens.
- Unknown presentation entities may recover/log but cannot fabricate a fire, mark, or damage intent.

**Network messages/version behavior:** no transport in M01. The future input frame carries aim plus ability-1 press sequence. Authoritative snapshots/events must preserve projectile ID/source, press sequence, cooldowns, pending buffer, mark owner/target/remaining ticks, raw-intent multiplier, and terminal contact.

**Persistence and idempotency:** none. Grave Mark state is ephemeral per LocalLab run. One accepted press sequence can fire at most once; a mark transition is uniquely described by `(tick, owner, target, source_projectile_id)`.

## Acceptance criteria

1. Validated content compiles exactly one Grave Mark definition: 150-tick cooldown, 5-tick global cooldown, 3-tick buffer, speed 12, range 11, radius 0.12, 28-tick maximum travel, `1.8W`, 120-tick duration, `+15%` marked-primary intent, one target, and consume-on-enemy-or-solid.
2. Rebind-ready sequenced right mouse fires immediately when ready, buffers only within the exact 3-tick readiness window, cannot leak through blocked input, and follows deterministic cooldown/GCD ordering.
3. Enemy contact emits raw intent `36`, applies/refreshes/replaces one owner-specific mark, and marked Pine Crossbow contact emits raw intent `23`; solid/range contact creates no mark and no path mutates health.
4. Stable-ID collision/projectile/mark/intent ordering and exact lifetime boundaries pass deterministic and adversarial fixtures.
5. LocalLab visibly presents the ability and active mark, and all local quality, content, deterministic, optimized-runtime, and evidence gates pass.
