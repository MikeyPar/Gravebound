# GB-M01-04C - Bell Proctor phases two, three, and breaks

**Status:** Completed and audited locally on 2026-07-11.
**Authorities:** GDD `ENC-003/004`, `COM-002/005/006/009`, Section 29; content specification `CONT-FP-005/008/010`; roadmap `GB-M01-04C`.
**Depends on:** `GB-M01-04B`.

## Implementation contract

- Thresholds at `70%` and `35%` cancel the prior timeline and every owned hostile exactly once, then enter a three-second no-attack break with `+20%` player damage received.
- Phase 2 uses the exact 10,000 ms fan/ring/cross schedule. Cross alternates cardinal and diagonal axes, width `1.0`, warning `900 ms`, active `500 ms`, raw physical Major `28`, once per cast, threat `12` per lane/max `24`, and excludes fan/ring impacts within `500 ms`.
- Phase 3 preserves both ordered ring previews/waits/gaps, fan and cross offsets, below-20% restart at `9000 ms`, and soft-enrage downtime-only reduction.
- Friendly damage resolves before boss scheduling each tick. Lethal damage clears boss projectiles/lanes without another hostile step and emits one defeat/reward transition.
- Victory summary reports clear/best time, damage taken, Tonic uses and lethal cause, resolves the boss reward without normal-wave modal actions, exposes primary Run Again, Escape-to-cleared-arena, and the cleared-arena pause action.

## Acceptance

Exact phase/cancel/break/preview/exclusion goldens, 20 complete deterministic boss runs, real lane/projectile damage, victory restart transaction, optimized active-boss and completion-summary evidence, and the full local gate must pass.
