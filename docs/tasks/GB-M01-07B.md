## Required behavior

- Compile exactly 12 fixed prototype equipment templates with no random affixes and no undocumented defaults.
- Reject any drift in ID, slot, rarity, effect set, operation, or value.
- Support the exact four crossbows, including Scatterbow three-bolt geometry and maximum two hits per target per release.
- Compile exact Grave Mark, Slipstep, armor, Still Eye, and Undertaker Knot overrides.
- Compile exactly five reward tables and exact ordered group/outcome payloads.
- Resolve normal-enemy equipment and Tonic checks independently.
- Resolve Wave 1 weapon plus Tonic; Wave 2 relic plus armor; Wave 3 Charm plus nonduplicate global equipment; boss three distinct global equipment plus two Tonics.
- Use deterministic named RNG and pin known-default-seed results.
- Route every reward through run-owned inventory so restart destroys it.
- Visibly demonstrate at least four distinct behavior changes.

## Acceptance criteria

1. Exact typed compilation and exhaustive drift fixtures cover all 12 items and five tables.
2. Multi-bolt emission is transactional; fixed vectors and two-hit target cap pass direct simulation tests.
3. Normal independent checks demonstrate all four possible outcome shapes.
4. Wave 3 and boss never duplicate equipment; boss yields exactly two consolidated Tonics.
5. Runtime evidence proves four live behaviors and the default-seed boss golden.
6. Cumulative local CI, strict content/lint, deterministic traces, optimized build, and clean inspected evidence pass.
