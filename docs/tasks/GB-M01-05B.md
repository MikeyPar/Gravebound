# GB-M01-05B - Telegraph, threat, and readability validator

## Task contract

**Feature IDs:** `GB-M01-05B`, `COM-005`, `COM-006`, `COM-009`, `CONT-010` through `CONT-013`, `CONT-FP-004`, `CONT-FP-009`
**Depends on:** `GB-M01-04A` and the shared damage/band boundary from `GB-M01-05A`
**Decision:** [ADR-012](../decisions/ADR-012-hostile-readability-manifest-and-exposure.md)
**Persistence:** none; deterministic validation and LocalLab presentation only.

## Required behavior

- Compile every validated hostile pattern into a sorted readability profile containing stable pattern/telegraph/audio IDs, origin, shape, color family, grayscale signature, band/outline, first/repeat warnings, lifetime, counterplay, threat, active-instance cap, and visual/audio priorities.
- Preserve the exact GDD warning minima: Chip `250/200 ms`, Pressure `400/300`, Major `650/500`, Severe `900/750`, Execution `1200/1000`; authored hostile milliseconds compile ceiling-to-tick.
- Require source silhouette or ground origin, exact kind-specific shape/counterplay/disposition, positive lifetime/threat/cap, compatibility tags, and Major+ `.warning.major` cue metadata.
- Validate at `4.5` speed, `0.25` hurtbox, `120 ms` RTT, no ability, normal corridor `0.80`, spawn reach `>=350 ms`, close-spawn `1.25 tile/750 ms`, Frostbind `4.0` overlap, and normal/boss aggregate caps `300/500`.
- Checked-sum aggregate threat and maximum active instances. Reject duplicates, mixed contexts, overflow, forbidden combinations, and aggregate cap excess with stable sorted diagnostics.
- Enforce `telegraph > hostile > friendly > loot > decorative` rendering and Major+ audio above ordinary warning audio.
- Give fan, ring-gap, and lane mechanics distinct grayscale grammar: tapered dart, hollow ring, and two-boundary lane band. Color cannot be the only differentiator.
- Repeated-use timing is legal only after the same pattern's first telegraph, resolve, and completion have all occurred. Wrong tick counts or illegal transitions cannot mutate exposure state.
- LocalLab must display the compiled profile/budget/priority data and authoritative observed first/repeat counts while protecting the central aiming corridor.

## Failure behavior

- Validation fails closed and returns every independent sorted/deduplicated diagnostic.
- No renderer, overlay, or audio system can make an invalid pattern legal.
- Exposure transition failure leaves the prior state unchanged.
- Missing future audio assets cannot be represented as successful playback; metadata validation and playback evidence remain distinct.

## Acceptance criteria

1. Individual and aggregate validators cover exact warning, geometry, compatibility, threat, cap, priority, shape, cue, and exposure rules.
2. The exact three FP enemy patterns compile to threat `33`, maximum active instances `20/300`, three ordinary warning cues, and distinct dart/hollow/band signatures.
3. Actual client layers preserve the validated priority order; hostile projectile families remain distinguishable in grayscale/high contrast.
4. Semantic optimized evidence shows both projectile grammars, lane-band trace, two or more observed warnings for every mechanic, and `REPEAT LEGAL`.
5. Focused tests, full local CI, strict content validation, deterministic traces, release build, inspected screenshot, documentation, and README handoff pass.
