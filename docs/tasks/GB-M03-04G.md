## Integrated lifecycle contract

- Use production framing, validation, authentication, selected-character binding, service coordinators, and PostgreSQL repositories; the fixture may seed approved disposable preconditions but may not call a coordinator in place of a lifecycle action that has a protocol surface.
- Compose character creation/selection, starter backfill, level/XP projection, durable reward placement, four-slot equipment, CharacterSafe/Vault transfers, explicit deliberate risk, and dormant danger-entry storage preflight.
- Every item mutation remains content-bound, source/version-bound, server-planned, idempotent, and atomic. The client never supplies a destination, security state, item version, resolved stat, placement map, or authoritative result.
- Reconnect and process restart reconstruct the exact selected character, level/XP, starter/reward identities and provenance, equipment, Belt/backpack/CharacterSafe/Vault projections, item/aggregate versions, and replay receipts.
- The harness advertises a disposable integration feature only. Normal Character Select `Play`, Realm Gate and Vault station interaction, Core promotion, and the normal player route remain disabled.
