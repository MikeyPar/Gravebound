**Status:** Complete - see [`GB-M03-05E-audit.md`](../milestones/GB-M03-05E-audit.md).

## Objective

Apply the three enabled Core Bargains to authoritative character combat and consumables: Cinder Hunger's direct-damage and maximum-health modifiers, Bell Debt's deterministic fifth-primary repeat, and Lantern Ash's potion-healing and active-belt constraint. Build the mechanics from the character's durable active-Bargain list without enabling purge, life cleanup, Core promotion, or the normal player route.

## Approved implementation contract

- One immutable compiled Core loadout accepts an optional Grave Arbalist Oath and zero to three active Core Bargains in acquisition order. No Oath is legal before level 10; the level-5 Bargain path must work.
- The atomic combat snapshot includes the positive Oath/Bargain aggregate version, ordered active Bargains with their immutable acquiring-offer revision, and the durable two-slot belt. Unknown, disabled, duplicate, reordered, corrupt, or revision-mismatched state fails closed.
- Runtime values come from validated typed `BargainBehavior` records. Simulation constants are validation targets, not a second authoring source.
- Cinder Hunger multiplies ordinary outgoing direct damage by `1.18` through the COM-002 attacker stage and maximum health by `0.88`. Primary, Grave Mark, and Nailkeeper trap damage are covered; the global `+50%` generic-damage cap and `0.70` maximum-health floor remain authoritative.
- Bell Debt reduces ordinary primary attack rate to `0.85` before Nailkeeper's `1.08` interval multiplier. Each accepted authoritative primary release advances one counter, including a legal miss; multibolt releases count once and generated repeats never count.
- The fifth release snapshots its aim and complete resolved projectile behavior, then repeats once at exactly `+300 ms` (`9` simulation ticks) from the character's live origin for `50%` damage after ordinary direct-damage resolution. It spends no cadence, cooldown, resource, or empowerment and cannot recurse.
- Bell counter state survives reconnect and room changes in the live aggregate. Validated checkpoint import/export and explicit acquisition, safe-transfer, death, and illegality reset/cancel seams are delivered here; durable lifecycle wiring remains `05F`.
- Lantern Ash composes base potion healing, named item effects, then `1.40` at the authored Bargain step and rounds once at the final healing boundary. Delivery remains `12` ticks and the shared cooldown remains `60` ticks.
- The normal belt has two active slots. Lantern Ash leaves index `0` active and index `1` stored, visible, and locked without moving or destroying contents. Empty index `0` never falls through to index `1`; an index-1 use returns a typed inactive-slot rejection.
- Fresh arena construction preserves immutable Oath/Bargain loadout configuration while resetting projectiles, cooldowns, Bell counter/pending repeat, and other transient state. The existing First Playable route remains unchanged and the normal Core route remains closed.
- Bell repeat and Lantern belt-lock presentation use distinct non-color text/shape/audio seams and retain identity under reduced motion. Final audiovisual and adversarial route closure remains `05F`.
