## Required behavior

- Strictly compile Slipstep as cooldown `240 ticks`, shared global cooldown `5`, input buffer `3`, distance `2.0 tiles`, travel `5 ticks`, travel step `0.4 tiles`, direct-damage reduction intent `2,500 basis points`, empowerment `45 ticks`, speed bonus `3,000 basis points`, pierce bonus `1`, and Exhaustion `45 ticks`.
- Bind Ability 2 through a replaceable action map: `Space` keyboard and left bumper gamepad defaults. A physical press increments once, short presses survive render/fixed-rate mismatch, menu-captured input cannot leak after the gate opens, and overflow/stale sequences fail closed.
- On accepted activation, lock normalized movement direction; when neutral, lock backward aim. Resolve the first segment before same-tick primary fire. Suppress ordinary walking while active.
- Sweep the player's `0.30 tile` circle through each segment. Stop exactly at first solid, terminate the cast, clear movement velocity, never cross a wall, and never treat an enemy as a movement solid.
- Expose 25% direct-damage reduction intent on each travel tick without implementing health, armor, resistance, invulnerability, death, or recovery.
- Apply Exhaustion and empowerment on activation. Both remain active for exactly 45 tick indices; expire before same-tick input/fire on the following boundary. Exhaustion rejects new movement abilities.
- Consume empowerment when the next primary is emitted. Apply `+30%` speed and one pierce to that immutable projectile. With Pine Crossbow this is `15.6 tiles/s` and at most two enemy contacts.
- Track sorted unique contacted-target IDs per piercing projectile. Resolve remaining travel by earliest contact, solid-first/stable-ID ties, monotonic contact ordinal, and once-per-target damage intent.
- Present a cyan/lavender Slipstep trail and empowered bolt using shape and luminance as well as hue. HUD must show cast/travel, cooldown, Exhaustion, empowerment, direct-reduction intent, empowered shot, and pierce contacts without covering the center aiming corridor.

## Legal transitions

- Ability input: `Idle -> PressObserved -> Began | Buffered(1..3) | ConsumedTooEarly | BlockedByExhaustion`; `Buffered -> Began | Replaced`.
- Travel: `None -> Began(5) -> Travelled(4..1) -> Completed`, or `Began/Travelled -> Collided -> None`.
- Cooldown: `Ready -> Cooling(240) -> ... -> Cooling(1) -> Ready`.
- Exhaustion: `None -> Active(45) -> ... -> Active(1) -> Expired`.
- Empowerment: `None -> Armed(45) -> ConsumedOnPrimary`, or `Armed(1) -> ExpiredBeforePrimary`.
- Projectile: `Spawned(empowered,pierce=1) -> HitEnemy(ordinal=0,continue,pierce=0) -> HitEnemyOrSolid(ordinal=1,terminal) | Expired`; a contacted enemy enters the immutable-for-that-projectile ignore set.

## Failure behavior

- Missing/duplicate/wrong-kind class or ability reference, any nonexact FP authored value, zero/invalid resolved value, stale/missing/overflow sequence, nonfinite aim/movement/collision state, unsorted ignore IDs, duplicate target contact, contact-ordinal overflow, modifier overflow, or entity-ID exhaustion fails closed.
- Avatar stepping is transactional: combat state and movement state both commit or neither commits.
- A presentation entity missing after a continuing/terminal collision may log and recover; it cannot fabricate movement, damage, pierce, or empowerment state.

## Tests required

- Unit/content: exact authored tuple and tick compilation; `180 ms -> 5 ticks`; malformed/reference/value drift rejection.
- Input: Space/left-bumper defaults, replaceability, one sequence per press, blocked-until-release, stale/overflow failure, 3/2/1 buffer and 4-too-early boundary.
- Movement: movement-direction and neutral-backward selection, five exact segments, final two-tile position, shell/pillar/corner contacts, collision-shortened completion, velocity reset, enemies nonblocking.
- Status: cooldown/GCD boundaries, 45-tick Exhaustion and empowerment lifetime, same-tick expiry-before-fire, Exhaustion rejection, consumption on emission.
- Projectile: exact 15.6 speed, pierce two targets once each, same-tick multiple contact, stable ID/tie ordering, contact ordinals, solid after first enemy, range expiry, no repeat hit.
- Determinism/failure: repeated trace pins position bits, timers, transitions, projectile state, contact order, and raw intents; invalid action/collision/ID paths roll back combat and movement.
- Visual/manual: optimized semantic capture proves trail, empowered projectile, two enemy contacts, HUD status/counters, collision geometry, and `HEALTH UNCHANGED`.

5. Unit, integration, adversarial, deterministic, content, optimized build/runtime, and inspected visual evidence gates all pass with README/development/audit documentation updated.
