# GB-M01-02E - Stillness and Focused

## Task contract

**Feature IDs:** `GB-M01-02E`, `CLS-020`, `SIM-005`, `SIM-010`, `COM-002`, `CONT-010`, `CONT-011`, `CONT-FP-001`
**Depends on:** `GB-M01-01B`, `GB-M01-02A`, `GB-M01-02C`, `GB-M01-02D`
**Decision:** [ADR-007](../decisions/ADR-007-stillness-focused-ordering.md)
**Content records:** `class.grave_arbalist`, `ability.arbalist.stillness`, `ability.arbalist.primary_crossbow`, `ability.arbalist.grave_mark`, `ability.arbalist.slipstep`, `item.prototype.weapon.pine_crossbow`
**Persistence:** none; LocalLab passive state is ephemeral.

## Required behavior

- Strictly compile Stillness as activation `18 ticks`, movement threshold `2,000 basis points`, projectile-speed bonus `1,000 basis points`, primary-damage bonus `800 basis points`, `break_on_damage=true`, and `break_on_slipstep=true`.
- Determine eligibility from the authoritative post-movement velocity for the current avatar tick. Compare its magnitude with `20%` of the resolved final movement speed. The comparison is strict: magnitude `< threshold` is still; magnitude `>= threshold`, including exact equality, is moving.
- Count one eligible tick per authoritative movement sample. Reset the counter to zero on any ineligible sample or Slipstep travel. Gain Focused on the eighteenth consecutive eligible sample, before primary resolution on that same tick.
- Keep Focused active without a duration while eligibility remains valid. Do not continue incrementing an unbounded counter after Focused is gained.
- Break Focused before primary resolution when post-movement velocity is at or above the threshold, or when Slipstep begins. Reset Stillness progress on either break.
- Expose a simulation-owned damage-received seam that breaks Focused and resets progress. `GB-M01-05A` must invoke it after a validated damage event is received and before any later same-tick primary resolution. This milestone does not invent health or hostile damage.
- Apply Focused to immutable primary projectile state at emission. Pine Crossbow speed becomes exactly `13.2 tiles/s`; raw primary damage becomes `22` by the shared integer half-up basis-point rule.
- Compose Focused and Grave Mark in authored order. Focused first resolves primary base damage `20 -> 22`; a marked-target collision then applies `+15%`, resolving `22 -> 25`. Do not recompute from `20` or combine percentages into one multiplier.
- Slipstep speed empowerment has precedence over Focused projectile speed when both windows are active; the speed bonuses do not stack. Focused may still contribute its primary-damage bonus when it remains active. A Slipstep begun on that tick breaks Focused before fire.
- Emit deterministic `Gained`, `BrokenByMovement`, `BrokenBySlipstep`, and `BrokenByDamage` transitions. Presentation consumes transitions and projectile provenance but never decides passive state.
- Present Stillness buildup, Focused state, Focused bolt provenance, raw damage, projectile speed, gain/break counts, and the deferred health boundary in the HUD without covering the center aiming corridor. Feedback must use shape and luminance as well as hue.

## Legal transitions

- Progress: `Reset(0) -> Counting(1..17) -> Focused(18)` on consecutive strict-below-threshold post-movement samples.
- Ineligible movement: `Counting(n) -> Reset(0)`; `Focused -> BrokenByMovement -> Reset(0)`.
- Slipstep: `Counting(n) -> Reset(0)` during travel; `Focused -> BrokenBySlipstep -> Reset(0)` when the cast begins.
- Damage: `Focused -> BrokenByDamage -> Reset(0)` through the explicit damage-received seam; damage while not Focused produces no false break transition.
- Primary: `Focused -> EmitPrimary(focused=true, raw=22, speed=13.2)`; gaining or breaking earlier in the same tick affects that emission immediately.
- Marked collision: `FocusedPrimary(base=22) -> MarkedPrimary(resolved=25)` using sequential half-up modifiers.

## Failure behavior

- Missing, duplicate, or wrong-kind passive reference; any nonexact First Playable authored value; zero activation; threshold outside `(0, 10000)`; zero modifier; missing required break flag; arithmetic overflow; or nonfinite movement/speed state fails closed.
- Avatar stepping remains transactional. An error in movement, passive evaluation, ability processing, modifier math, projectile allocation, or collision cannot partially commit movement or combat state.
- Presentation may recover from a missing visual entity, but it cannot fabricate Focused state, a transition, projectile provenance, damage, or speed.
- Until `GB-M01-05A`, no runtime debug action may pretend that health damage occurred. Tests may call the explicit break seam directly and must label that boundary.

## Tests required

- Unit/content: exact `600/2000/1000/800/true/true` authored tuple; `600 ms -> 18 ticks`; passive-reference and value-drift rejection; definition invariant failures.
- Threshold/order: strict below, exact equality, and above threshold; post-movement rather than input sampling; reset of partial progress; gain exactly on tick 18; gain-before-fire on tick 18.
- Breaks: movement-break-before-fire; Slipstep-begin-break-before-fire; progress reset during Slipstep travel; damage seam breaks only active Focused and resets progress.
- Projectile math: Pine Crossbow exact `13.2 tiles/s`; raw `20 -> 22`; Focused projectile provenance; Slipstep speed-precedence/nonstacking; modifier overflow failure.
- Composition: same-tick lower-ID Grave Mark and later primary collision produce primary base `22` then marked resolved `25` in stable projectile/contact order.
- Determinism/failure: repeated trace pins Focused progress, transition ticks/kinds, projectile provenance, speed bits, and raw intents; invalid paths roll back the whole avatar tick.
- Visual/manual: optimized semantic capture proves 18-tick gain, Focused bolt, exact HUD speed/damage, transition diagnostics, collision target, and the explicit no-health/deferred-damage boundary.

## Explicitly out of scope

- Health mutation, hostile damage delivery, armor/resistance/barrier order, death, Red Tonic, enemy AI, Still Eye equipment override, oaths, networking, persistence, production audio, and final art.

## Acceptance criteria

1. Strict validated content compiles the exact immutable Stillness definition and fails on any First Playable drift.
2. Eighteen consecutive strict-below-threshold post-movement samples gain Focused before same-tick fire; equality, faster movement, Slipstep, and received damage reset or break it with deterministic transitions.
3. Focused Pine Crossbow primaries emit with exact `13.2 tiles/s` speed and raw damage `22`; a same-tick earlier Grave Mark composes that primary to resolved raw damage `25` in stable order.
4. State ownership remains in `sim_core`, damage integration remains an explicit `GB-M01-05A` seam, and client presentation visibly communicates buildup, active state, provenance, and breaks without becoming authoritative.
5. Unit, integration, adversarial, deterministic, content, optimized build/runtime, and inspected visual evidence gates pass, with the milestone audit and Current Next Step updated.
